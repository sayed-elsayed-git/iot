Background / Scenario

With IoT devices and IoT networks getting popular, technologies for IoT devices are also improving rapidly. IoT devices are no longer limited to sensors and actuators. They may have the capability to think and act accordingly with environmental changes, thanks to the development of artificial intelligence (AI) and machine learning (ML).

Instructions

Part 1: Consider an IoT Application with AI/ML Technology

In this part, you will list the functions and features of a smart home thermostat and a controller device with capability of self-learning, making decisions based on the environmental changes, and acting accordingly.

Questions:

List the desired features and function for such a device.

Answer Area

The list will vary. Possible items may include:

• Understanding commands from human beings, such as “Turn up/down temperature.”
• Automatically adjusting house temperature according to environmental changes.
• Communicating with cloud computing for self-learning and algorithm improvement.

List the factors that may influence the perception of temperature.

Answer Area

Answers will vary. Possible items may include:

1. What is a comfortable temperature? Different people may react differently to one set of temperatures.
2. Current room temperature
3. Moisture/humidity
4. Proximity to the air vent
5. Time of the day

List the ways the smart device can get information about those factors.

Answer Area

Answers will vary. Possible items may include:

1. Internal clock to keep time/date
2. Thermostat and moisture sensor
3. Commands from human being
4. Network access for constant weather forecasting

Part 2: Design Components Required for an IoT Application with AI/ML Technology

In this part, you will explore and design the functions of the key components required for the smart thermostat/controller.

Questions:

What are the key components for a smart thermostat/controller?

Answer Area

Answers will very. Possible item should include:

1. Temperature and moisture sensor
2. Access to a cloud computing application through a home gateway
3. Microphone and speaker to interact with a person
4. Microprocessor/Controller to turn on/off the heater or AC unit
5. Power connection and battery backup

List the process and operation of the smart thermostat/controller?

Answer Area

Answers will vary. Possible items should include:

1. The cloud computing has a model for normal room temperature through analysis of data from a large sample set.
2. Initially the device will adjust room temperature according to the model with respect to the region and historical data from neighborhood.
3. The initial factors may include time of day and current room temperature.
4. Room temperatures may be adjusted if commands are received from a person.
5. The data is sent to the cloud computing application for constant fine tuning of the model.
