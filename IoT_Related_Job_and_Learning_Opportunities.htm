Lab - IoT Related Job and Learning Opportunities

Objectives

The purpose of this lab is to explore job and learning opportunities in the ever-evolving world of the Internet of Things.

Part 1: Research Jobs in the Field of Internet of Things

Part 2: Determine the Skill Set Required for Each of These Positions

Part 3: Explore Learning Opportunities

Background / Scenario

The Internet of Things is growing exponentially. New technologies and applications are being developed on a regular basis and this creates an abundance of new job opportunities each with its requisite skill sets. It is important to stay current with the skills required for this evolving job market and to take advantage of the many available learning opportunities currently available.

Required Resources

=   Device with internet access

Instructions

Part 1: Research Jobs in the Field of Internet of Things

a.     Open up a web browser and search for “types of jobs with learning opportunities in the internet of things.”

b.     You can include other relevant words, like “lifelong learning” or even a job title that you may be already interested in.

Question:

Identify and document three job designations that you are interested in.

Answer Area

Answers will vary. Some examples can be web development engineering, software engineer, and IoT Security Engineer.

Part 2: Determine the Skill Set Required for Each of These Positions

Use a search engine to research job titles that you have identified as interesting. Search for the skill sets required for these job titles. Pay special attention to skills required for multiple positions.

Answer Area

Answers will vary. For an IoT Security Engineer, he will need skills in IoT, Security, USB, networking, network security, manufacturing, and certificates, such as CISSP, CCSP, CISM.

Part 3: Explore Learning Opportunities

Use a search engine and other available resources and research learning opportunities available within the sphere of those job titles. Document all the information gathered from this research.

Answer Area

Answers will vary. For example, online courses are available on the topics of IoT, programming languages, data management, networking, network security, and AI.

Reflection

Are there any patterns observable in the IoT job market?

Answer Area

The IoT has become an enabling technology across all industries. Positions are available for IT people with strong programming, network, and security skills who can help enhance this technology. There is also a need for a general understanding of how to best make use of this new technology.
