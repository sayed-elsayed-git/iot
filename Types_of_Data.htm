Lab - Types of Data

Objectives

In this lab, you will record a few objects or places that could use sensors to collect data and determine if the collected data is sensitive.

Background / Scenario

It is important to recognize where sensors are being used in our world today and what types of data they are collecting. We need to determine if the collected data is sensitive in nature. If it is sensitive, is it PII? Who might benefit from stealing it?

Instructions

Select 3 or 4 objects or places that could contain sensors. List the types of data that could be collected by the sensors. Determine if any of the collected data is sensitive. Record your finding in the table below.
